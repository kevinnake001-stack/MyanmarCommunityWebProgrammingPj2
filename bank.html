Bank Account & Money Transfer Guide

Opening a bank account in Indonesia is simple. You can visit local branches such as BCA, Mandiri, or BNI with your passport, student card, and residence permit. Most Myanamr students prefer using BNI because of its easy mobile banking features, you can pay in IndoMart using BNI mobile and ATM access everywhere around Cikarang.

  • Requirements: Passport, Student ID, Residence Permit
  • Recommended Banks: BCA, Mandiri, BNI
  • Mobile Apps: BCA Mobile, Mandiri Online
