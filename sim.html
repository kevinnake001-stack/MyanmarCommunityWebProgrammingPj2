SIM Card & Internet Guide

Getting a SIM card in Indonesia is very easy. You can buy it at the airport, convenience stores, or phone shops around Cikarang (including Meikarta and Lippo areas). The two most popular SIM cards for students are Telkomsel and Indosat (IM3).
