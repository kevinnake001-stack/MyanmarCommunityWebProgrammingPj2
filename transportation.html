Transportation Guide

There are many transportation options here for students to shop or hang out. For short trips around Cikarang, you can use the Gogo app for motor e-bikes, or Gojek for taxis — which works like Grab in Myanmar. If you want to go to Jakarta, you can travel not only by car but also by train. These options make daily travel easy, fast, and affordable.

Type Example Average Cost Payment Method
Bus SBH to NBH, Movie Land - only Free -
Taxi Go-Jek At least 15000 RP, around 3000 MMK Cash and online payment
Train Cikarang to Jakarta Around 6000 RP, around 1500, 2000 MMK Pay with Go-Pay
